Pen’s Bungalow, establishing in 1986. We are a seaside resort just a few steps from the sea. Ideal for families, friends, and couples. Enjoy our tasty local and international food at the seaside restaurant. Drink and relax with infinity pools view. We also offer a one-stop service at the reception where you can ask for everything! Such as bike and car for rent, taxi service, boat trips, laundry, and money exchange.
